yam Yet Another Metronome

A musician-grade floating metronome that stays on screen while you practice. Sub-millisecond precision. Beautiful themes. Zero distractions.

Download Free View Source

Available for macOS, Windows, and Linux

Built for Musicians

🎯

Sub-Millisecond Precision

Hybrid sleep + spin-wait timing engine running on a dedicated thread. No jitter, no drift.

🪟

Always-On-Top Widget

Compact floating widget stays visible while you practice with your DAW, tabs, or sheet music.

🎨

Beautiful Themes

Multiple dark and light themes with accent colors that match your style.

⌨️

Global Shortcuts

Control playback, BPM, and subdivisions without switching windows.

🧘

Zen Fullscreen Mode

Immersive fullscreen with visual effects — Focus, Pulse, Gravity, Sweep, and Cosmos.

🏋️

Speed Training

Automatic BPM ramping to build speed gradually. Set start, target, and increment.
